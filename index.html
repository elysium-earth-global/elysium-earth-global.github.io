Purpose

Why Elysium exists

Elysium Earth Global is a voluntary, non-profit and charitable organisation founded in Oslo in May 2026. Its role is simple: to hold the funds, the accountability and the legal responsibility that transparent humanitarian work requires so that the projects on the ground can focus entirely on delivering results.

“To support and finance humanitarian projects that promote education, welfare and sustainable development for vulnerable children and families in developing countries.” — Founding charter, Oslo, 6 May 2026

Education

Schooling, materials and learning environments that give children a real path forward.

Welfare

Health, nutrition and safety for children and the families that care for them.

Sustainable development

Community-led projects designed to keep working long after the funding cycle ends.

How it works

One umbrella. One operational partner. Direct impact.

Elysium Earth Global does not run projects itself. It commissions a qualified operational partner ELYRA Earth under a formal cooperation agreement, and oversees how every krone is used.

The umbrella

Elysium Earth Global

Receives donations and grants, holds legal and financial responsibility, and reports as a registered Norwegian non-profit.

→
The operational partner

ELYRA Earth

Runs the transparency platform connecting sponsors directly with community projects, under a separate cooperation agreement.

→
The destination

Community projects

Village-level initiatives in education, welfare and development with progress sponsors can follow directly.

Accountability by design

What the umbrella structure guarantees

I

Registered and regulated

Founded in Oslo and registered with the Brønnøysund Register Centre, including the Register of Non-Profit Organisations (Frivillighetsregisteret).

II

Built for tax-efficient giving

The organisation is structured to meet the requirements for recognised charitable status in Norway, so that gifts can qualify for available donor benefits.

III

Separation of duties

Governance and funding sit with Elysium; operations sit with ELYRA Earth. A formal agreement defines mandates, reporting and child-protection standards.

Governance
